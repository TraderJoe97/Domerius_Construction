About Us

Founded in 2016 by Henry Sechele, Domerius Services started in architectural services, construction management, project management, and material management. Over time, we expanded into building residential and commercial properties, with the mission to bring your dreams to life. Our goal is to provide excellent, sustainable solutions across South Africa.

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to make the process of building and designing a home or commercial building seamless and efficient. We prioritize customer satisfaction through innovation and sustainable practices.
